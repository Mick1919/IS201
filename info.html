Class Info

Our class focuses primarily on test taking strategies and study techniques. Secion-specific content is also covered, but the main purpose is giving students the skills necessary to maximize test-taking efficiency and study effectively in the areas they are weak.

Each class will focus on a different section of the ACT. English, Math, Reading, and Science sections will be covered in succession, along with study techniques.

The cost is $60 per student, and registration is in the Bountiful High School business office
