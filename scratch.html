Why Nature Rocks



Reasons Why Nature Rocks:


  1. Outdoor Sports!
    • Skiing
    • Rock Climbing
    • Mountain Biking
    • Boating
    • Hiking
  2. Without it, we would die
  3. Going outside has serious health benefits!
    • lower risk of heart disease and high blood pressure!
    • lower cortisol levels (less stress)
    • higher levels of vitamin D!
  4. Mountains

 

 

 

 

I love the outdoors. Ever since I was a kid, my parents have taken our family to do everything from camping to river rafting. We started these activities because that was what my parents were into when they were dating, but we kept doing them because we came closer together as a family. Nature holds a special place in our hearts.
